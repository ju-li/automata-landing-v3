    Can I use Automata even if I don't have a CRM?

    Automata offers an open API allowing its users to freely access it. For the less tech-savvy, the free version comes with Google Sheets integration, allowing users to schedule and batch send personalized texts.

Starting January 2024, Automata will be available to Salesforce and Hubspot users among other CRMs. We also offer custom integrations for pre-paid commitments - Please contact us if you're interested.
