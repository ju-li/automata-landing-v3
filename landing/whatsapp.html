    FAQ

    Can I use Automata even if I don't have a CRM?

    Automata offers an open API allowing its users to freely access it. For the less tech-savvy, the free version comes with Google Sheets integration, allowing users to schedule and batch send personalized texts.

    Is Automata compatible with all sales and enrichment tools?

    Automata is currently fully integrated with Follow Up Boss and can easily be set up with Zapier. Starting January 2024, Automata will be available to Salesforce and Hubspot users among other CRMs. We also offer custom integrations for pre-paid commitments - Please contact us if you're interested.

    Can I use Automata for other forms of texting?

    Automata can also be used to automate texting with iMessage, SMS, Telegram, and Android Messages.

    How can I cancel my Automata subscription?

    Users can easily cancel by themselves through their user portal. We don't require any commitment and if you're not satisfied with the product you get your money back no questions asked.
